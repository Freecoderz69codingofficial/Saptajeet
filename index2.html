The Rulers of Delhi Sultanate

Delhi first became the capital of a kingdom. Under the Tomara Rajputs, Delhi first became the capital of a kingdom. In the middle of the twelfth century, Tomara Rajputs were defeated by the Chauhans of Ajmer. Under the rule of both, Tomaras and Chauhans, Delhi flourished as an important trading city. Dehliwal coins were minted and circulated in day-to-day transactions. Many temples were built by rich Jaina merchants who lived there.

The Delhi Sultanate was founded in the beginning of the thirteenth century. Under the Delhi Sultanate, Delhi got transformed into a commercial capital that controlled vast areas of the subcontinent.

The Delhi Sultans built vast areas in and around the place we now know as Delhi.
